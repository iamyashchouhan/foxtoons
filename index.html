About

Foxtoons Club is a collection of programmatically generated NFTs on the blockchain. The 1st generation consists of 3333 randomly assembled Foxtoons, picked from a pool of over 500,000 possible outcomes. Foxtoons Club come with a unique variation of bodies, faces, and hats. Each Boodle is randomly generated & provably unique.

Frequently asked quetions

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for “Non-fungible Token”. These tokens are unique and cannot be replaced for something else. They are one of a kind digital item that users can buy, own, and trade. Some NFTs only purpose is to exist as artwork, others offer additional utility like exclusive access to gated sections of websites & Discord servers, or participation in an event. We like to think of NFTs as a collectable piece of art that doubles as a “members only” access key.

What is Metamask?

Metamask is a web3 cryptocurrency wallet where you can store Matic. It is required to purchase and mint Boodles Club. Having a wallet gives you a unique Matic address (i.e. 0x1234…ABCD), this is where your NFT will be sent upon successful purchase. Metamask also doubles as a gate function.
